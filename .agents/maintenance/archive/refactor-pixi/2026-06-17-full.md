### Looks bad but fine

- `staleTime: 0` on resources — intentional, interpolated from WS
- Resources/Crowns in Zustand not TanStack Query — design decision, performant for high-frequency ticks
- 24 mutations without optimistic UI — most are server-critical ops
- WorldMapScene as single factory — idiomatic Pixi pattern, closures maintain coherence

## Changes applied

1. **ws-bindings.ts**: Added `armyInventory` + `population` invalidation to `applyBattleSent` and `applyScoutSent` (2 lines each). Aligns with what the REST mutations already do in `onSettled` and with the pattern in `applyReinforcementSent`.

2. **ws-bindings.test.ts**: Added 5 new `describe` blocks covering the previously untested handlers:
   - `applyBattleSent` — snapshot creation + armyInventory/population/openExpeditions invalidation
   - `applyScoutSent` — snapshot creation + armyInventory/population/openExpeditions invalidation
   - `applyScoutReported` — RESOLVED→RETURNING phase transition with timer
   - `applyScoutReturned` — RETURNED phase + cleanup delay + armyInventory invalidation
   - `applyExpeditionReturned` — RETURNED phase + cleanup delay + armyInventory/resources invalidation

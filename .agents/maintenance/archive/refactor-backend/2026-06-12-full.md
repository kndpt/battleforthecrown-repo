### Selected theme: B3 — batch village→userId lookup in outbox dispatch

**Evidence:**
- `dispatchPendingEvents` fetches up to 100 events per poll (`event-outbox.service.ts:60-64`).
- Each `notify*` method called `getUserIdByVillage` → `prisma.village.findUnique` individually.
- `getUniqueUserIdsByVillages` compounded this with `Promise.all` of N individual lookups for caravan/reinforcement multi-recipient events.
- Worst case: 100 events × 1 query = 100 round-trips per outbox poll cycle (~1s).

**Fix:**
- Pure helper `collectVillageIdsFromPayload` extracts scalar (`villageId`, `defenderVillageId`, etc.) and array (`reinforcementOriginVillageIds`) fields from parsed payloads.
- `prefetchVillageUserIds` runs one `findMany` before the dispatch loop.
- Scoped `villageUserIdByVillageId` Map cleared in `finally` block.

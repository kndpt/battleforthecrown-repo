# Gameplay Overview

## Overview

This document defines the overall vision and main gameplay loops for _Battle for the Crown_. It is designed for machine readability to assist AI agents in understanding the core systems and interactions within the game. The focus is on high-level strategy, player goals, and system relationships.

## Core Objectives

- **Macro-Strategy**: Emphasize broad strategic decisions over micromanagement.
- **Simplicity**: Maintain clear and straightforward mechanics to facilitate player comprehension and AI interpretation.
- **Clarity**: Provide transparent feedback and outcomes for player actions.

## Primary Gameplay Loops

- **Economy Loop**: Manage resources, production, and trade to sustain your kingdom.
- **Military Loop**: Recruit, train, and deploy units to defend and expand territory.
- **Conquest Loop**: Plan and execute territorial expansion through strategic battles and diplomacy.
- **Retention Loop**: Maintain population happiness and loyalty to prevent unrest and loss of control.

## Design Principles

- **Decisions**: Focus on meaningful player choices that impact long-term outcomes.
- **Feedback**: Deliver timely and informative responses to player actions.
- **Retention**: Encourage continued engagement through rewarding progression and challenges.

## Relationships

This system integrates closely with:

- **Population System**: Influences growth, happiness, and workforce availability.
- **Buildings System**: Provides infrastructure that supports economy and military.
- **Power System**: Governs resource allocation and strategic capabilities.

## Constraints

- Does not include micro-decisions such as individual unit control.
- Excludes talent trees and detailed character customization.
- Avoids complex AI opponent behaviors beyond strategic objectives.
